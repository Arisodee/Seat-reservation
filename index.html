SEAT RESERVATION SYSTEM

What We Do

We offer a seat reservation system to any organization in need of one. Our Major target audience is churches since with the COVID period its become difficult to control numbers of people coming to church and keep a record of who sat where for contact tracing purposes

Our system requirs users to enter their bio data then select number of seats needed. It will then allow you to tick the seats required and will indicate in red seats already taken.

It will help churhces collect informtaion of who attended which service and where they sat as give members the benefit of reserving a seat prior to the service and select where to seat.
